Production facility SAIPL

With our abundant experience and skilled technology in producing high manganese steel, high chromium, alloy steel, carbon steel, heat-resistant steel and other alloys we offer quality casting at very competitive prices.
  • Melting - Induction Furnace Crucible - 2200kg, 1500kg, 500kg
  • Moulding - Chemical Bonded Sand System Complete With Continuous Mixer & Mechanical Sand Reclaimation Arrangements.
  • Treatment - Oil and Gas Fired Heat Treatment Furnaces - Having Auto Control & Temperature Recording Graph With Water, Air, & Oil Quenching Arrangements. 
  • Testing Facility - Complete Equipped Laboratory with  ARL make Spectrometer, Microscope & Other Instruments for Metallurgical, Physical & Sand Testing.
  • Pattern Shop - Equipped Pattern Shops with bend saw, we have more then 5000 Patterns in storage which are all devloped inhouse.
  • Material Processing - Grinding Fettling shops, Hydraulic Press of various size, Shot Blasting Machine (72' Table Dia) along with Fully Equipped Machine Shop Facilities (Own And Ancillaris) consisting of Plano millers, Lathes , VTL and Drills
