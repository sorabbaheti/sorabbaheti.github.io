Our Journey Through the Time

1991                           

Company Inception

Standard Alloys India (P) Ltd, is established in 1991 at Ajmer as a small Foundry with crucible furnace and 5 labours with aim to provide quality casting to cement and mining Industry.

1995

Induction Furnace Installation

The expansion was undertaken in 1995 with an Induction furnace of 500 Kg capacity was installed.

2002

Increasing Melting Capacity

Addition of 1 MT crucible further expanding our portfolio of products   

2006                         

Second Melting facility

We started with our second manufacturing unit in ajmer. With Addition of 2 MT crucible in our foundry and to meet the growing demand of cement industry

2009

No Bake Sand Moulding

In 2009 we improved our moulding methodolgy from CO2 moudling to Alpha set based moulding process. Along with it we added another crucible of 2000 Kg. 

2012

Machine Shops

We added with Machine shop facility with VTL machines, Plano Millers, lathes and drill machines enabling us to machine diameter upto 6500mm.

2019

Aquired Surge Alloys Pvt Ltd

This aquisition of surge alloys pvt ltd trippled our produciton capcity to 700 MT per month and exceeded our casting capacity upto single piece 2500 kg along with added heat treatment and maching facilities.
